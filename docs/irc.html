Perl IRC

The primary support channel is the IRC channel of the Padre developers. It is located on the irc.perl.org server and it is called #padre. You can use either an IRC client if you have one on your computer or the web-based Mibbit IRC client.

Other support channels are also available.

Please select a nickname and a channel and click on Connect.
